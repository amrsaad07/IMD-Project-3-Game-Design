About the game

In the lighthearted and entertaining 2D game Hungry Hero, your character gets bigger each time it consumes a treat. The concept is simple to comprehend. You navigate the map, gather food, and see your character grow bigger with each bite. Every round feels different as you get bigger because the game gets more thrilling and a little more difficult to navigate. It is modeled after the traditional "eat and grow" game genre, but it has a friendlier, more lighthearted feel. Your objectives are to continue eating, survive, and strive to be the biggest hero on the screen.
